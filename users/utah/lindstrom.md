Events:
- Hired as Associate Professor of Computer Science, effective 1 January 1977.
- Tenure awarded in the Department of Computer Science effective 1 July 1981.
- Sabbatical leave during 1983–84 academic year.
- Promotion to rank of Professor denied on 6 April 1984.
- Promotion to the rank of Professor of Computer Science, effective 1 July 1987.
- Sabbatical leave during autumn, winter, and spring quarters of the 1990–91
  academic year at Cornell.
- Sabbatical leave during 1997–98 academic year to research at Carnegie Mellon
  University and visit INRIA in France to participate in the SOR and ATGC
  projects.
- Sabbatical leave during 2004–05 academic year to work with colleagues at NASA
  Ames Research Center.
- Early retirement effective 1 July 2007, requested 12 October 2006.
- Designated as Professor Emeritus in the School of Computing, effective 1 July
  2007.
