Meeting     David Evans         Oct 30. 1975         Dick R.
            Martin Newell

Subject:    Completion of Frame Buffer / Picture System Contract

E&S agrees to supply:
            Partitioned Picture System Control
            MAP Speed-up ECO
            Extra 8K x 36 Refresh Buffer
            18 bit DMA address register ECO
            NOP ~ Truck Mode ECO

U of U agrees to fulfil its commitment to supply
E&S intl system software. This will consist
of basic user software for the Frame Buffer,
and demonstration programmers for the Frame Buffer
and Picture System. Some basic systems
for Picture System will be included.
U of U will assemble package and such software
for presentation to E&S.

We agree that, notwithstanding, any other prior
understandings or agreements, on completion of
the above actions both sides will be considered
to have satisfied their the contract.


Accepted David C Evans 30 Oct 1975
Accepted Martin E Newell. 30 Oct 1975.
