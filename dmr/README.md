  Written after being notified of the archived letter by Lars Brinkhoff on 11
  July 2023 [[TUHS](https://www.tuhs.org/pipermail/tuhs/2023-July/028603.html)].

`dmr2` also contains "The UNIX Time-Sharing System" (`paper/`, modified 26
November 1973), "UNIX Assembler Reference Manual" (`aman/`, modified 8 December
1973), "The Unix I/O System" (`iosys`, modified 9 December 1973), "Regenerating
System Software" (`direc`, modified 15 November 1973), a document describing the
directory layout (`direc1`, modified 15 November 1973), a document on
restrictions on forming types (`retric`, modified 8 December 1973), a list of
several points made in the SOSP'73 paper (`ct`, modified 4 December 1973), and
several miscellaneous programs.
