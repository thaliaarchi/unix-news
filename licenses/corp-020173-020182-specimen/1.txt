                            ARTICLE II

            GRANTS OF RIGHTS TO USE LICENSED SOFTWARE

  2.01  (a) AT&T, at the request of LICENSEE, grants to LICENSEE, for itself
and its SUBSIDIARIES, a personal, nontransferable and nonexclusive right to use
the LICENSED SOFTWARE, solely for its own or its SUBSIDIARIES’ internal
business purposes and solely on DESIGNATED CPUs.

  (b)  A single back-up CPU may be used as a substitute for a DESIGNATED
CPU without notice to AT&T during any time when such CPU is inoperative
because it is malfunctioning or undergoing repair, maintenance or other
modifications.

  (c)  LICENSEE may at any time notify AT&T in writing of its designation by
location, type and serial number of any replacing or additional CPU that is to
become a DESIGNATED CPU and any CPU to be replaced. Any such designation
shall become effective on agreement thereto in writing by AT&T and receipt by
AT&T of the appropriate fee, if any, specified in Section 3.02.
